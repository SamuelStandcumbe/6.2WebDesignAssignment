Jack Jetson has always had a deep love for classic cars, and there's one in particular that he can't get enough of – the iconic Mini. What started as a personal hobby has now evolved into a thriving project, with Jack dedicated to restoring and rejuvenating these timeless machines. Operating out of his spacious home garage, Jack not only brings new life to these beloved cars but also offers a range of services, including expert re-sprays in his dedicated re-spray shop. With several restoration projects underway and an archive of completed work, Jack’s ambition is to one day turn his passion into a full-time business. In the meantime, he’s keen to share his work and insights with a wider audience. Alongside his restoration work, Jack is an active organiser of local classic car rallies, where car enthusiasts come together to show off their restored vehicles, share stories, and enjoy the camaraderie of like-minded individuals. Whether you’re a local looking to join in on the next rally, or someone from further afield wanting to learn more about these events, you’ll find all the information you need, from dates and routes to registration details right here!




As someone who truly understands the craftsmanship behind every restoration, Jack's rallies have become well-known in his area, attracting classic car lovers from all over. He’s involved in every aspect of the events, from coordinating the logistics to curating routes that showcase the beauty of the local scenery while providing a thrilling driving experience for participants. Jack’s rallies aren’t just about the cars; they’re about building a community, fostering friendships, and keeping the spirit of classic cars alive.
